💡 Quick Life Tips

🍳 Non-Stick Pan Care

Never use metal utensils on non-stick pans. Hand wash with soft sponge to extend lifespan by years.

👕 Remove Wrinkles Fast

Hang wrinkled clothes in the bathroom while showering. Steam removes wrinkles in 10 minutes.

📱 Free Up Phone Storage

Delete old screenshots and duplicate photos first. They usually take up several GB of space.

🏃 Stay Motivated

Put your workout clothes next to your bed. You're 3x more likely to exercise in the morning.

🧊 Ice Cube Hack

Freeze leftover wine in ice cube trays for cooking. Perfect for sauces and deglazing pans.

💰 Track Every Expense

Write down every purchase for 30 days. You'll be surprised where your money actually goes.

🌿 Keep Herbs Fresh

Store fresh herbs like flowers in a glass of water. Cover loosely with a plastic bag. Lasts 2 weeks!

📧 Email Efficiency

Check email only 3 times a day: morning, noon, and late afternoon. Boosts productivity by 25%.
